MALLEODYN S3

Dismountable ankle brace for dynamic mobilisation.The innovative ankle joint brace can be easily dismounted in three steps in line with the course of therapy. In the acute phase, it stabilises, relieves and cools the ankle according to the RICE rule (rest, ice, compression, elevation). Due to the flexible inner-shoe-like construction and the easy handling, it can be used for secondary prevention after the 3 dismounting steps. The QR code in the product packaging gives you access to therapy exercises.

INDICATIONS

  • Acute ankle injuries
  • Acute capsule ligament instabilities and ruptures
  • All indications in which stabilization or movement restriction is required of the ankle joint with subsequent functional mobilization
  • Chronic capsular ligament instability
  • Limitation of the forward displacement and tilt of the talus
  • Post-operative foot & ankle
  • Prevention of ligament instabilities
  • Sprains of the foot & ankle
  • Supination traumas

EFFECTIVENESS / TECHNOLOGY

  • Avoidance of 'window edema' due to closed orthosis construction
  • Limitation against talus feed and tilting via 8-bridle and distal click lock
  • Limitation against talus feed and tilting via 8-bridle and distal click lock
  • Stabilisation of the ankle in inversion and eversion
  • Stabilisation of the ankle joint via 3-point principle
  • Therapy-compatible resting according to the RICE rule (integrated cooling pad)

  • Anatomically shaped side splint - easy to disassemble with one click
  • Antibacterial effect of the inner padding
  • Bi-elastic inner pad for pressure-free ankles
  • Click fasteners: One-time adjustment of the straps for quick application with two clicks
  • Due to very flat construction wearable in every shoe
  • Figure 8 strap dismountable
  • Heel width adjustable via plantar strap: optimal heel mount
  • High wearing comfort: anatomical 3D shell construction combined with bi-elastic spacer fabric
  • Maximum breathability: hydrophilic bi-elastic spacer fabric with carbon finish absorbs moisture and transports it outwards

SP AIR WALKER

Lower limb and foot brace for immobilisation.The integrated air chamber system with pump allows the adjustment of the optimal fit. The low weight and the air-permeable frame construction ensure a high level of wearing comfort. The toe protection is individually adjustable.

INDICATIONS

  • Achilles tendon rupture
  • Ankle fractures
  • Distal fibular fracture
  • Post-operative foot & ankle
  • Severe ankle sprains
  • Severe ligament and soft tissue injury
  • Stress fracture of the foot & ankle
  • Tarsal bone and mid-foot fracture

EFFECTIVENESS / TECHNOLOGY

  • Excellent wearing comfort thanks to low weight and air-permeable frame construction
  • Inflating air chamber inside the shoe ensures secure hold and stabilisation
  • Possibility of plantar flexion adjustment by means of optionally available heel wedges, e.g. for rupture of the Achilles tendon (article no. 27966)
  • The custom-adjustable pressure in the air chamber generates compression and has a swelling-reducing effect

  • Adjustable and removable toe protection
  • Shell construction for the secure immobilisation of the lower leg and foot area at a 90° position

  • Shock-absorbing and slip-preventing sole with rolling aid
  • The frame of the orthosis can be securely closed and adjusted with Velcro fasteners
  • The shaft edge is flexible thanks to multiple breaks, avoiding pressure points
