Thumb and Foot pain

RHIZO-HiT

Textile brace for the metacarpo-phalangeal and carpo-metacarpal joint.With mouldable aluminium inlay and closure principle with slip-stop.

INDICATIONS

  • Arthrosis of the radial carpus and MCP 1
  • Conservative / post-operative thumb and finger
  • Injuries, tendon instabilities in the area of the metacarpo-phalangeal and carpo-metacarpal joint (e.g. skier’s thumb)
  • Mobile phone thumb
  • Moderate to severe rhizarthrosis with marked subluxation with/without hypermobility of the carpo-metacarpal joint
  • Thumb fractures
  • de Quervain's tendovaginitis stenosans (mobile phone thumb)

EFFECTIVENESS / TECHNOLOGY

  • Limitation of freedom of movement in the wrist
  • Preservation of grip function
  • Stabilisation of the metacarpo-phalangeal and carpo-metacarpal joint

  • Anatomically mouldable aluminium inlay
  • Circular strap with forearm sling and block to prevent sliding out

THUMB SADDLE JOINT BRACE

Brace for fixation of the metacarpo-phalangeal and carpo-metacarpal joint.

INDICATIONS

  • Arthrosis of the radial carpus and MCP 1
  • Conservative / post-operative thumb and finger
  • Navicular pseudo-arthrosis
  • Partial immobilisation of the radial carpus of the metacarpo-phalangeal and carpo-metacarpal joint in cases of saddle joint arthrosis
  • Ulnar lateral ligament lesion of MCP 1 (e.g. skier's thumb)

EFFECTIVENESS / TECHNOLOGY

  • Partial immobilisation of the metacarpo-phalangeal metacarpo-phalangeal and carpo-metacarpal joint as well as the carpus
  • Preservation of grip function

  • Enables air circulation
  • Prevention of waterlogging
  • Thermally mouldable
  • Wrist remains freely mobile

ACCESSORIES

Under glove/cushion insert optionally available

RHIZOSPLINT

Rhizarthrosis brace for immobilisation of the metacarpo-phalangeal and carpo-metacarpal joint.

INDICATIONS

  • Arthritis / arthrosis of the metacarpo-phalangeal joint, carpo-metacarpal joint
  • Conservative / post-operative thumb and finger
  • Ligament instability in the area of the thumb
  • Previous fracture in the area of the metacarpo-phalangeal joint, 1st metacarpal, carpo-metacarpal joint
  • Rhizarthrosis
  • Tendon inflammation in the area of the thumb

EFFECTIVENESS / TECHNOLOGY

  • Counteraction of subluxation
  • Free mobility of the radial wrist
  • Immobilisation of arthrotic and unstable carpo-metacarpal joint
  • Immobilisation of hypermobile metacarpo-phalangeal joints

  • Sweat-absorbing padding inlay
  • Thermally mouldable material
